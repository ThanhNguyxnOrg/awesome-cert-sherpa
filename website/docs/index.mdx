# 🏔️ Awesome CertSherpa

A community-driven collection of study resources, practice tools, and curated guides for professional IT certification exams — vendor-neutral and open to all.

Whether you're chasing your first cloud cert or stacking advanced security credentials, this hub organises everything you need in one place so you can **study smarter, not harder**.

:::tip 🎯 New here?
Start with the [Cloud](/docs/categories/cloud) or [Security](/docs/categories/security) categories — they're our most popular and have the most resources!
:::

## 📖 What You'll Find Here

- 📚 **Curated resource lists** — handpicked study guides, video courses, labs, and cheat sheets for each certification domain.
- 📂 **Category overviews** — browse by discipline (Cloud, Security, Networking, DevOps, and more).
- 💡 **Community-contributed tips** — exam-day strategies, study plans, and "lessons learned" from people who passed.
- 🧠 **Practice engine** — an interactive quiz with 1,590+ original questions across 14 certifications. [Try it now!](/practice)

## 🗂️ How This Site Is Organised

| Section | What's inside |
|---------|---------------|
| 📂 **Categories** | High-level domains — Cloud, Security, Networking, DevOps, Data & AI, Linux, PM & ITSM. |
| 🎓 **Certifications** | Individual cert pages with resource tables, difficulty ratings, and community notes. |
| ✍️ **Field journal** | Announcements, contributor spotlights, and exam-prep deep dives. |
