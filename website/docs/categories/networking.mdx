# Networking Certifications

Networking certifications prove your understanding of how data moves across systems — from LAN fundamentals to enterprise routing, switching, and wireless design. This category covers vendor-neutral and vendor-specific tracks alike.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Foundational** — CompTIA Network+, Cisco CCT, and entry-level credentials.
- **Associate & professional** — CCNA, CCNP, JNCIA/JNCIP, and other vendor tracks.
- **Expert-level** — CCIE, JNCIE, and architect-tier certifications.
- **Wireless & specialty** — CWNA, CWDP, SD-WAN, and network automation certs.
- **Study resources** — packet tracer labs, subnetting drills, video courses, and community guides.
