# 🛡️ Security Certifications

Cybersecurity certifications demonstrate your competency in protecting systems, networks, and data from threats. This category spans entry-level awareness certs through advanced offensive and defensive specialisations.

:::tip 🔥 Did You Know?
CompTIA Security+ is one of the most widely recognized entry-level security certifications and is approved for **DoD 8570/8140** baseline requirements — making it a must-have for government and defense cybersecurity roles.
:::

## 📖 What You'll Find Here

- 🔰 **Foundational certs** — Security+, SSCP, CC, and other entry points into the field.
- 🏛️ **Mid-level & management** — CISSP, CISM, CISA, and governance-focused credentials.
- ⚔️ **Offensive security** — OSCP, CEH, GPEN, and red-team certifications.
- 🛡️ **Defensive & cloud security** — CCSP, GCIH, CySA+, and blue-team tracks.
- 📚 **Curated resources** — labs, CTF platforms, study plans, and practice exams.
