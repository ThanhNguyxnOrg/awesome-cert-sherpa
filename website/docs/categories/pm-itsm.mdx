# Project Management & IT Service Management Certifications

PM and ITSM certifications validate your ability to lead projects, manage services, and deliver value through structured frameworks — from agile delivery to enterprise service operations.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Project management** — PMP, CAPM, PRINCE2, PMI-ACP, and other PM frameworks.
- **Agile & Scrum** — CSM, PSM, SAFe Agilist, and related agile credentials.
- **IT service management** — ITIL 4 Foundation → Master, VeriSM, and SIAM.
- **Curated resources** — exam simulators, framework comparison charts, and study group directories.
