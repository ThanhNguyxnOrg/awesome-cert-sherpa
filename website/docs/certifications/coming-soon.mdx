# Certifications — Coming Soon

Individual certification pages are on the way. Each page will include:

- **Exam overview** — code, cost, duration, passing score, and renewal policy.
- **Resource table** — official guides, community courses, practice exams, and labs ranked by the community.
- **Difficulty rating** — crowd-sourced difficulty and estimated study time.
- **Tips & gotchas** — exam-day advice contributed by people who passed.

## Want to Help?

The fastest way to get a cert page created is to **open an issue** on the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/ThanhNguyxn/awesome-cert-sherpa/issues) with the cert name and any resources you'd recommend. We'll turn it into a page.

:::info Roadmap
We're building cert pages incrementally. Popular certifications (AWS SAA, CISSP, CCNA, CKA, RHCSA) will be prioritised first based on community demand.
:::
